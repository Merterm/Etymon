Proto-Turkic: *Erneg
Altaic etymology: Altaic etymology
Meaning: edge (of a dish)
Russian meaning: край, кромка (посуды)
Turkish: ernek (dial.)
Tatar: irnäw
Middle Turkic: ernek (Pav. C.)
Turkmen: erŋek
Kirghiz: erdȫ
Kazakh: ernew
Bashkir: irne- 'to border'
Karakalpak: ernek
Comments: ЭСТЯ 1, 301. The root is attested late and may be theoretically derived from *Erin 'lip' (v. sub *i̯ằré); however, the latter can also mean 'edge (of a dish)' in some languages and one can suspect that PT actually merged *Erin 'lip' and *Erin 'edge', while the form *Erneg was derived only from the latter.
