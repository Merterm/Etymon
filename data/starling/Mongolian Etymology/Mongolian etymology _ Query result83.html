Proto-Mongolian: *seɣül
Altaic etymology: Altaic etymology
Meaning: oar
Russian meaning: весло, рулевое весло
Written Mongolian: seɣül (L 683)
Khalkha: sǖl
Kalmuck: sǖl
Comments: KW 342. The root is homonymous with *seɣül 'tail' (v. sub *si̯ū́bu), but certainly different historically; although now it is almost obsolete (but noted both in Lessing's and Ramstedt's dictionaries), it is certainly archaic.
