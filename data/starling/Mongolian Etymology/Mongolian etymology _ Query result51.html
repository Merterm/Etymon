Proto-Mongolian: *kelbeɣür
Altaic etymology: Altaic etymology
Meaning: chock, boot-tree
Russian meaning: колодка
Written Mongolian: kelbegür (МХТТТ)
Khalkha: xelbǖr
Buriat: xelberi
Dagur: kelbej-
Comments: Mong. > Orok qalụmụri 'board'. The word is formally derived from kelbe- 'to shape', whence also kelberi (L 446) 'shape': external evidence, however, strongly suggests that 'boot-tree' must have been the original meaning, and 'shape' is a secondary developed abstract meaning.
