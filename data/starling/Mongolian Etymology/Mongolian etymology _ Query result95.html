Proto-Mongolian: *tarija-n
Altaic etymology: Altaic etymology
Meaning: crops
Russian meaning: зерно
Written Mongolian: tarijan
Middle Mongolian: tarijan (HY 4), tarijat (pl.) (SH), tariān (IM)
Khalkha: taria
Buriat: taŕā(n)
Kalmuck: tarān (KW), tärɛ̄n (КРС)
