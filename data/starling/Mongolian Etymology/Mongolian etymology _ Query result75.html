Proto-Mongolian: *nomu- /*neme- / *nima-
Altaic etymology: Altaic etymology
Meaning: gentle, meek, quiet, mild
Russian meaning: спокойный, кроткий, мягкий, нежный
Written Mongolian: nomuqan, nomuj (L 591), nemegün (L 574), nimaɣun
Middle Mongolian: nomuqan (HYt), nomuɣan (MA)
Khalkha: nomxon
Buriat: nomoj, nomxon
Kalmuck: nomɣǝn, nemǖn
Ordos: nomoxon
Dagur: nomūkuŋ, -n (Тод. Даг. 158: nomxon, nomukan); nomehon (MD 200)
Shary-Yoghur: nomxon, nomukan (MGCD nomoGon)
Monguor: nomu
Comments: KW 275-277, 279, MGCD 513. The deriving stem is WMong. nom, Kalm. nom 'mildness, friendliness' (KW 279). Mong. > Evk. nomokōn, Man. nomoxon etc., see Doerfer MT 57, Rozycki 164.
