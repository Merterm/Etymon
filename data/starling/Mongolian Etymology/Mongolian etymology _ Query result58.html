Altaic etymology: Altaic etymology
Meaning: to foam, swell up
Russian meaning: пениться, вздуваться
Written Mongolian: kögege-, köge-, kögere- (L 478)
Middle Mongolian: kɛhɛ- (IM), ku- (MA)
Khalkha: xȫ-
Buriat: xȫ-
Kalmuck: kȫ-

Dongxian: kue- (MGCD ko-)
